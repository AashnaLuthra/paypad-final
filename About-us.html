Moving into a new house should be special, not expensive

When tenants move in their dream house, they have a lot of expenses and important financial decisions to make. At PayPad we will believe that expensive security deposits should not be one of them. PayPad enables tenants to move in deposit-free while giving better security from damages and unpaid rent to Landlords/Property Managers.

PayPad is on a mission to make renting affordable in India

We wanted to create a financial product that was better than a expensive security deposit because affordable housing is important. We do not want unnecessary expenses to be the reason why you do not move into your dream house..
